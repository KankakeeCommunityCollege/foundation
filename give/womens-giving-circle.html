Women’s Giving Circle: Educate, Empower and Endeavor

Grant assistance for second year students in need

KCC students who are in their second year can receive a grant when facing barriers to completion. This program requires a faculty referral and a faculty mentor to ensure that students stay on the path to cross the finish line of graduation. Assistance can include childcare, eldercare, food, healthcare, transportation and housing.

Give Now or contact the KCC Foundation.

All contributions are tax deductible under the provisions of §501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code.

For more information, call the Foundation office at 815-802-8950 or kccfoundation@kcc.edu.
