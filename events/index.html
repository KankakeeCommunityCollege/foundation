The KCC Foundation held its second Annual Give Day on August 27. This peer to peer fundraiser asked KCC alumni, donors and friends to give and encourage others to give. Over $20,000 was raised to assist student and college needs.

At the annual Scholarship Event, scholarship recipients and those who donated to their scholarship have the opportunity to meet.

President's Circle Dinner

KCC’s President’s Circle recognizes donors who have contributed annual gifts of $1,000 or more. These gifts—in the form of annual donations, endowed scholarships, planned endowments and support for the college’s capital development—make a lasting impact for KCC students.

Sincere thanks to our Leadership Sponsor, Nucor Steel, and all of our event sponsors. New Beginnings was a wonderful success! All proceeds will benefit KCC students and emerging needs. Click on the New Beginnings logo (at left) to view event photos.
