At the annual Scholarship Event, scholarship recipients and those who donated to their scholarship have the opportunity to meet. Recipients share their appreciation with donors and talk more about their plans for the future. Donors are able to see firsthand the impact their donations made and how their contributions transformed students’ lives.

KCC Foundation Scholarship Breakfast

The KCC Foundation invites all 2019-20 Foundation Scholarship recipients and donors to the Scholarship Breakfast on Thursday, Oct. 17 from 7-9 a.m. in the College Center at KCC's Riverfront Campus, 100 College Drive, Kankakee.

  • Check in at 7 a.m.
  • Breakfast at 7:30 a.m.
  • Program begins at 8 a.m.

This event is only open to 2019-20 KCC Foundation Scholarship recipients and donors.

For more information, please contact the Foundation Office at 815-802-8252. More information about KCC Foundation scholarships is at foundation.kcc.edu/scholarships.
