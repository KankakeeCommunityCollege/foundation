KCC Foundation Annual Scholarship Event

KCC Foundation Scholarship Event

At the annual scholarship event, scholarship recipients and those who donated to their scholarship have the opportunity to meet. Recipients share their appreciation with donors and talk more about their plans for the future. Donors are able to see firsthand the impact their donations made and how their contributions transformed students' lives.

The 2026-27 scholarship event will be held on Wednesday, Aug. 5, 2026.

Due to limited seating capacity, student guests are not permitted for this event. Attendance is reserved only for student recipients and their scholarship donors.
