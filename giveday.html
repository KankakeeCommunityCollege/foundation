The KCC Foundation will hold its first Give Day on August 27! This peer to peer fundraiser asks KCC alumni, donors and friends to give and encourage others to give. The world is changing rapidly and we must keep pace to ensure student success! The two initiatives below are our Give Day funding priorities.

The Equity, Diversity and Inclusion (EDI) Center will provide services and activities that will create a climate of intentional interaction. Together, students will utilize critical thinking, expand awareness and increase overall cultural competency.

Laptops and WiFi Units are needed because many of our students have no access to technology and need resources to complete their coursework.

Sponsors of $1,500 or more are invited to attend the closing reception on August 27 at 5 p.m. on Main Campus.

100% of the proceeds from this event will support students and college initiatives!

The first $5,000 we raise will be matched, dollar for dollar, thanks to a generous donor!
