GIVE DAY

#strongtogether

GIVE DAY is the one day that alumni, families, students, staff, faculty, and friends unite to support one common goal: supporting KCC's mission—Enhancing Quality of Life through Learning.

{% comment %}

The KCC Foundation would like to acknowledge that this year's event is happening during a very challenging time in our community. Given the unprecedented changes occurring on our campus and the impact of the pandemic on our community, it feels right to unite our college community and supporters in a common effort. We also know that our students need our support more than ever. 100% of your gift will have a direct and immediate impact on KCC students.

GIVE DAY FUND
Funds will be used to address students' emerging needs. For example, many students need technology like laptops or personal WiFi, others need emergency assistance for things that financial aid won't cover, some face food insecurity and other personal hardships. Whatever the need, your contribution to the GIVE DAY fund will be there to help.

Student Success Stories

When you donate to KCC GIVE DAY #strongtogether, you will be a part of a student's success story:

“My name is Kyla, I am a former Kankakee Community College student. I am proud to say that I am a three time scholarship recipient. I majored in Business Communications and received my Associates of the Arts degree in early 2021.

Community college has transformed my life in a way that I have yet to find words for. I started my journey as a homeless high school student. Carrying a past of abuse and neglect I had no hope for the future until KCC gave me a purpose.

KCC allowed me to further my education while I began my career. The flexibility of my education allowed me to work my way up at Nucor, from a 17 year old intern into the administrator I am today. I was so moved by what Kankakee Community College had to offer, that I am working towards becoming an adjunct professor as I continue my career at Nucor.

KCC donors have given me the foundation to build a life for myself, to build a future. I reflect on the time prior to my enrollment as a reminder of who I was, and who I can be. The meaningful donations from people like you could mean a new beginning for someone just like me. Through education we can empower each other and our community.”

Kyla Brychta
