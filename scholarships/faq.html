KCC Foundation Scholarships Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are KCC Foundation Scholarships?

KCC Foundation Scholarships are financial awards available to students attending Kankakee Community College. These funds have been donated by donors seeking to help KCC Students achieve their financial dreams. These scholarships help cover tuition, fees, and other educational expenses like books and supplies.

Who can apply for KCC Foundation Scholarships?

Scholarships are available for new, returning, and transfer students at KCC. Eligibility criteria vary but may include financial need, academic achievement, career goals, and community involvement. Everyone is encouraged to apply for scholarships that will be attending KCC.

Are KCC Foundation Scholarships only for full-time students?

No. Many scholarships are available for both full-time and part-time students. Be sure to check the specific eligibility requirements for each scholarship.

When is the deadline to apply?

The deadline for KCC Foundation Scholarships is May 1

What documents do I need to submit?

  • A completed application
  • Letters of recommendation(s)
  • Transcripts if applicable

Do I have to submit the FAFSA?

While not all scholarships require a FAFSA, it is highly recommended. Many scholarships consider financial need, and FAFSA information helps determine eligibility.

How will this affect my Pell Grant?

Foundation Scholarships are utilized before your Pell grant. If you are awarded Pell this may increase any Pell refund.

Can I apply for multiple KCC Foundation Scholarships?

Yes! When you complete the general scholarship application, you will be automatically considered for all scholarships for which you qualify.

Do I need to write an essay?

The general scholarship application includes short essay questions. You may write your essay questions in a word document and then copy and paste it into your application when you are ready to submit.

Selection & Awarding

How are scholarship recipients selected?

Scholarship readers evaluate applications based on financial need, academic performance, essay quality, and overall application strength. Some scholarships have specific eligibility requirements.

When will I find out if I received a scholarship?

Scholarship award notifications are typically sent out in June. Be sure to check the email you applied with and the scholarship portal for updates.

If I receive a scholarship, how will I get the money?

Scholarship funds are applied directly to your KCC student account in the Fall and Spring semesters to cover tuition and fees. If additional funds are available, you may use it for books or supplies in the KCC Hammes Bookstore.

Do I need to maintain a certain GPA to keep my scholarship?

Some scholarships require students to maintain a specific GPA or meet other academic requirements. Be sure to review the terms of your award.

Can I lose my scholarship?

Yes. Scholarships may be revoked if you fail to meet the eligibility requirements, drop below the required enrollment status, or do not maintain satisfactory academic progress. Please contact the KCC Foundation office if anything changes that may affect your scholarship so we may help.

Other Questions

Can I use my KCC Foundation Scholarship for another school?

No. KCC Foundation Scholarships can only be used for tuition and fees at Kankakee Community College.
