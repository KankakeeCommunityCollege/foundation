Century Scholars: The Power of Tradition

Established in 1984, this scholarship provides students with a full ride to cover tuition, books and fees.

The Century Scholars Scholarship is awarded to students who demonstrate outstanding academic performance as well as strength of character. Century Scholars are recognized at the annual Scholarship Event in October.

Century Scholars Recognition Society

The Century Scholars Recognition Society is made up of caring individuals wanting to make a difference. Donors support three scholarships that provide funding for tuition, books and fees for one year to outstanding KCC sophomore students.

Century Scholars recipients are chosen from the top divisional scholarship candidates.

Help students reach their educational goals

Your gift to the Century Scholars program provides tremendous advantages to KCC students. Tuition, fees and books only represent a portion of the actual cost of a student's education.

Your gift will allow students to reach their educational goals and impact on the socio-economic vitality of our community.

Become a supporter

Donors are included in the Century Scholars Recognition Society through an annual donation of $125 per individual or $250 per couple. To be included in this prestigious recognition society, you may mail your gift to the Century Scholars Recognition Program c/o KCC Foundation, 100 College Drive, Kankakee, IL 60901.

Give Now

As a supporter, you'll be invited to the Scholarship Recognition Dinner in October, where you will learn first-hand how you are making a significant impact on the scholarship recipients.
